First Major Project with Shree Cement
2012-2014

In 2012, Jatin Enterprises embarked on its first major project with Shree Cement, Jaipur. The project focused on manufacturing and supplying large-scale school furniture, marking a significant milestone in our journey. This endeavor showcased our dedication to quality craftsmanship and our ability to meet the rigorous demands of commercial contracts. The successful completion of this project bolstered our reputation within the industry and laid the foundation for future growth and expansion.

International Expansion with South Africa's Education Department
2014-2016

Building on our local success, from 2014 to 2016, Jatin Enterprises ventured into the international market through a collaboration with Lucky Export and South Africa's Education Department. This partnership involved the manufacturing and supply of school and college furniture for educational institutions in South Africa. It represented our first foray into international projects, highlighting our capability to deliver tailored solutions across borders while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. This period marked a pivotal moment as we solidified our presence in global markets and diversified our portfolio.

Project with UltraTech Cement, Aditya Birla Group
2016-2017

In 2016, Jatin Enterprises secured a project with UltraTech Cement, a prominent entity within the Aditya Birla Group. The project focused on manufacturing and supplying bunk beds, showcasing our versatility in providing specialized furniture solutions for industrial environments. This collaboration underscored our ability to adapt to diverse client needs and further solidified our reputation as a reliable partner in the manufacturing sector.

Expansion into B2C Segment
2017-2018

By 2017, Jatin Enterprises took a strategic step towards diversifying its business model by entering the business-to-consumer (B2C) segment. We launched our own offline stores and began supplying various retail outlets in Jaipur. This initiative aimed to enhance our local presence and offer direct access to our comprehensive range of furniture products to residential and commercial customers alike. The expansion into the retail sector marked a significant milestone in our growth trajectory, allowing us to cater directly to the needs and preferences of local consumers.

International Project with MS International , USA
2019-2021

In 2019, Jatin Enterprises expanded its global footprint through a significant project with MS International, a leading company based in the United States. The project involved the supply of marble stands, showcasing our capability to deliver specialized furniture products to international markets. This collaboration further solidified our reputation as a trusted supplier of innovative and high-quality furniture solutions on a global scale, while also opening new avenues for future international partnerships and growth opportunities.

Project with Tesca Tech
2022-2023

In 2022-2023, Jatin Enterprises partnered with Tesca Tech, a prominent company based in Jaipur, for a project focusing on manufacturing and supplying school furniture. This collaboration strengthened our local partnerships and underscored our commitment to providing high-quality furniture solutions for educational institutions in our home city. The project reaffirmed our reputation for excellence and reliability in meeting the specific requirements of our clients within the local market.
